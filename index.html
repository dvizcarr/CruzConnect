Where Santa Cruz Comes Together.

  • Rideshare

    The Rideshare tab is for connecting with other students and SC residents for rideshares and carpools. Share the journey, reduce your carbon footprint, and build connections on the road.

  • Tutoring

    The Tutoring tab is for learning and teaching. Offer your knowledge or get the help you need by connecting with tutors and learners. Expand your skillset and help others through an exchange of knowledge.

  • Recreation

    The Recreation tab is for meeting new people. Whether it's through hiking, playing sports, or exploring new activities, use this space to create memories with new people with Santa Cruz as your playground!

  • Lost & Found

    The Lost and Found tab is for helping others find what they lost. Share information about lost items or connect with those who have found something you thought you'd never retrieve.
